Submissions

We invite authors to submit unpublished papers (8-page ICCV format) to our workshop, to be presented at a poster session upon acceptance. All submissions will go through a double-blind review process. All contributions must be submitted (along with supplementary materials, if any) at this CMT link.

Submission guidelines : http://iccv2021.thecvf.com/node/4#submission-guidelines
